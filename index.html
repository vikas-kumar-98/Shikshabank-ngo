Our Programs

Comprehensive educational initiatives designed to address diverse learning needs

Free Educational Books For All

At ShikshaBank, we believe that education is a fundamental right, not a privilege. Our "Free Educational Books for All" program is born from this core belief, striving to dismantle the barriers that prevent children from accessing their school books.

Learn More

Free Education For All

At ShikshaBank, we believe that every child deserves access to quality learning, regardless of their location or circumstances. Our "Free Education for All" program is a pioneering digital initiative designed to deliver comprehensive educational support directly to students, leveraging the power of online learning.

Learn More

Free Plantation

At ShikshaBank, our commitment to a brighter future extends beyond education to encompass a healthier, greener planet. Our "Free Plantation" program is a cornerstone of this vision, actively working to combat climate change, enhance urban biodiversity, and inspire environmental stewardship across Delhi and beyond.

Learn More

Creating Lasting Impact

At ShikshaBank, our journey is defined by a singular purpose: to create profound and lasting positive change in society. As we look towards 2025, we are proud to consolidate our efforts and showcase the transformative impact achieved through our three interconnected flagship programs: "Free Educational Books for All," "Free Education for All," and the "Free Plantation" Initiative.

Each program is meticulously designed to address critical societal needs, fostering a cycle of empowerment and sustainability that will resonate for generations.
