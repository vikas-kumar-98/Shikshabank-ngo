Early Childhood Education Program

Building strong foundations for lifelong learning

Program Overview

Our Early Childhood Education program focuses on children aged 3-6 years, providing them with a strong foundation for formal education through play-based and activity-based learning approaches.

Key Objectives

  • Develop cognitive, social, and emotional skills
  • Prepare children for formal schooling
  • Promote holistic development through play
  • Engage parents in early childhood development

Implementation Strategy

We establish early learning centers in underserved communities, staffed with trained educators who use child-centered teaching methodologies. The program includes regular health check-ups and nutritional support.

Impact Metrics

5,200+ Children Reached
150 Learning Centers
92% School Readiness
12 States Covered

Program Duration

3-year program cycle with continuous enrollment

Geographic Reach

Currently operational in 12 states across India, with focus on rural and tribal communities
