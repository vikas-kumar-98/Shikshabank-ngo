Transforming Lives Through Education

Since our inception, ShikshaBank has been committed to creating measurable, sustainable impact in the lives of underprivileged children across India. Our evidence-based approach ensures that every intervention creates lasting change.

We believe in transparency and accountability, which is why we rigorously measure our outcomes and share our results with our supporters. Here's how your support is making a difference.

Our early education program focuses on building strong foundations for children aged 3-6 years, preparing them for formal schooling and lifelong learning.

Success Story: Anaya's Journey

Anaya joined our early education program as a shy 4-year-old with limited exposure to structured learning. Within a year, she developed confidence, curiosity, and foundational literacy skills. Today, she's excelling in her primary school and dreams of becoming a teacher.

Success Story: Rahul's Transformation

Rahul, from a remote village, had never used a computer before joining our digital learning program. Today, he codes simple applications and helps his community with digital tasks. He's now pursuing a degree in computer science.

Girl Education Impact

85% School Completion

Success Story: Priya's Empowerment

Priya was supposed to be married at 16, but our scholarship program enabled her to continue her education. She became the first engineer from her village and now mentors other girls in her community.

Our Journey of Impact

Key milestones in our mission to transform education in India

2010

Foundation

ShikshaBank was established with a mission to provide quality education to underprivileged children. Started with 3 learning centers reaching 150 children.

2013

Expansion

Expanded to 5 states, reaching 5,000 children. Launched our first vocational training program for youth.

2016

Girl Education Focus

Launched dedicated girl education program with scholarships and mentorship. Reached 10,000+ students across India.

2019

Digital Initiative

Introduced digital learning labs and coding programs. Reached milestone of 50,000 children educated since inception.

2023

National Recognition

Received national education award. Reached 75,000+ children across 25 states with comprehensive education programs.
