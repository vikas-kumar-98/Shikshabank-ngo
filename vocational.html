Building Skills for Sustainable Livelihoods

Our Vocational Training program equips youth and adults with market-relevant skills to secure employment or start their own businesses, breaking the cycle of poverty through practical education.

We focus on developing skills that are in high demand in local and global markets, providing comprehensive training that includes technical expertise, soft skills, and entrepreneurship development.

Our Vocational Training Approach

Comprehensive skill development for employability and entrepreneurship

Industry-Relevant Skills

Our courses are designed in consultation with industry experts to ensure graduates have skills that employers actually need.

Job Placement Support

We provide placement assistance, resume building, and interview preparation to help graduates secure employment.

Entrepreneurship Training

For those interested in starting businesses, we offer entrepreneurship development and micro-enterprise support.

Women-Centric Courses

Special programs designed for women, focusing on skills that can be practiced from home or in flexible work environments.

Digital Literacy

All vocational courses include basic digital skills training to prepare students for the modern workplace.

Industry Partnerships

We collaborate with companies for internships, on-the-job training, and direct recruitment of our graduates.
