Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about contacting us

What is the best way to get a quick response?

For the quickest response, we recommend using our live chat feature during business hours (Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 6 PM). For non-urgent matters, email typically receives a response within 24 hours.

How can I volunteer with ShikshaBank?

We welcome volunteers in various capacities! Please select "Volunteer Opportunity" as your subject when filling out the contact form, or email us directly at volunteer@shikshabank.org with your area of interest and availability.

Can I visit your education centers?

Yes, we welcome visitors to our education centers with prior appointment. Please contact us at least one week in advance to schedule a visit. Due to the sensitive nature of our work with children, all visitors must complete a brief orientation.

How can my organization partner with ShikshaBank?

We partner with corporations, educational institutions, and other NGOs. Please select "Partnership" as your subject in the contact form, and our partnership team will reach out to discuss potential collaboration opportunities.

Do you have regional offices I can contact?

Yes, we have regional offices in 8 states across India. While our headquarters is in Maharashtra, you can reach our regional teams by emailing region@shikshabank.org (replace 'region' with the state name in lowercase).
