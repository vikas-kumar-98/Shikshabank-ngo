Our Story

Founded in 2010, ShikshaBank.org emerged from a simple yet powerful belief: every child deserves access to quality education, regardless of their socioeconomic background. What started as a small initiative in a single community has now grown into a nationwide movement.

Our journey began when our founder, Dr. Anjali Sharma, witnessed firsthand the educational disparities in rural India. Determined to make a difference, she gathered a team of passionate educators and social workers to create sustainable educational solutions.

Today, we work across 18 states, partnering with local communities, government bodies, and corporate sponsors to create lasting educational impact. Our approach combines traditional teaching methods with innovative technology to reach children in the most remote areas.

Our Mission & Vision

Guided by our core principles, we work towards creating a more educated and equitable society.

Our Mission

To provide quality education to underprivileged children by creating sustainable learning ecosystems, empowering communities, and breaking the cycle of poverty through knowledge and opportunity.

Our Vision

We envision an India where every child has access to quality education, regardless of their background, and where education becomes the primary catalyst for social transformation and economic development.

Our Values

These core values guide everything we do at ShikshaBank.org

Compassion

We approach our work with empathy and understanding, recognizing the unique challenges faced by each child and community.

Integrity

We maintain transparency in all our operations and are accountable to our donors, partners, and the communities we serve.

Innovation

We continuously seek new and effective ways to address educational challenges and improve our programs.

Collaboration

We believe in the power of partnerships and work closely with communities, governments, and other organizations.

Our Journey

Key milestones in our growth and impact over the years

2010

Foundation

ShikshaBank.org was founded with the mission to provide education to underprivileged children in rural Maharashtra.

2013

Expansion

Expanded operations to three additional states, reaching over 2,000 children through our programs.

2015

Digital Initiative

Launched our Digital Learning Program, bringing technology-enabled education to remote areas.

2018

National Recognition

Received the National Education Excellence Award for innovative educational practices.

2021

COVID Response

Adapted our programs during the pandemic, reaching 15,000+ children through remote learning initiatives.

2023

Current Reach

Now operating in 18 states, impacting over 15,000 children annually through various educational programs.
